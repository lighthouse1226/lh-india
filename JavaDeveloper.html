Lighthouse Software (www.lighthousesoftware.com) is a fast-paced software development company seeking a Java Developer to join our team. This is a full-time, permanent position based in the Twin Cities. We are searching for a full-stack developer who also has solid experience with a responsive scripting framework. We use a wide range of technologies on our projects, for this particular opportunity we are looking for someone with experience in core Java, Spring, and Angular.


Summary of Responsibilities
You will work closely with a small team of five developers on a large-scale application replacement effort within the Financial Services space. This year-long effort seeks to replace a number of existing, custom file-ingestion applications into one unified platform with an Angular front-end. The application being constructed is a version 1 prototype that will demonstrate to the business owners that a new solution using current technologies will allow the users more flexibility and quicker turn-around on new features.

You will act as a implementer of features on this new application, and will work closely with the lead developer and architect to collaborate on design decisions and problem solutions. Beyond your work in development, you’d also interact with client SMEs on a day-to-day basis. The team works in an Agile cadence to deliver both front- and back-end features.

This is great opportunity for anyone with solid Java and Angular experience who wishes to keep building their skills on enterprise-scale application development.
